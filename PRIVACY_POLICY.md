# Privacy Policy — Flipbook Waitroom Monitor

**Last updated:** April 25, 2026

## Overview

Flipbook Waitroom Monitor is a Chrome extension that displays your real-time queue position on [flipbook.page](https://flipbook.page). Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

This extension does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

- **No personal information** is collected (name, email, etc.)
- **No analytics or tracking** services are used
- **No data is sent** to any third-party server
- **No cookies** are created by this extension

## How It Works

The extension runs only on `flipbook.page` and makes requests to `https://flipbook.page/api/waitroom` using your existing browser session to retrieve your queue status. All data is processed locally in your browser and is never stored or transmitted elsewhere.

## Permissions Used

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `notifications` | Show a browser notification when you are admitted from the queue |
| `host_permissions` (flipbook.page) | Inject the queue monitor widget and fetch queue status from the Flipbook API |

## Third Parties

This extension does not share any data with third parties.
